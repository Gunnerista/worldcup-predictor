How to read this
  • The bracket shows the single most-likely path (the favorite advances at each tie). It is the modal scenario, not a certainty — the board below gives every team's full odds.
  • Advance % = win + half of the draw (knockouts have no draws). Extra time / penalties are treated as a coin-flip — validated on 2018&2022 knockout games.
  • Completed ties are fixed to their real result; only undecided ties are simulated.
  • Inputs are pure team data — goals, xG, ELO, form-based strength. No player, injury, or coaching guesses.
  • Limitation: champion odds do not yet model strength uncertainty, so they are mildly overconfident for outsiders. See Evaluation for details.
